# What “Полный маршрут” delivers

## Input
- actual exam subjects and scores;
- regions/cities and relocation limits;
- budget/paid preference;
- applicant achievements as facts, not guessed points;
- benefits/quotas/target admission;
- preferred fields and excluded fields.

## Engine
1. Reject incompatible exam combinations.
2. Check every subject against the university minimum.
3. Calculate achievements separately for every university using its rules and cap.
4. Estimate three bands from historical cutoffs and current seat data: safe, realistic, ambitious.
5. Diversify the list so it is not ten nearly identical programs.
6. Build a priority order: strongest desired option first; safety options lower.
7. Attach deadlines, required documents and source links.

## Output
- compatible programs with every official code;
- applicant score and university-specific achievement score;
- previous cutoff and 2026 budget seats;
- confidence/status of every fact;
- ordered application plan;
- calendar of deadlines;
- document checklist;
- change alerts when an official document is replaced.

The route is decision support, not a guarantee of admission.
